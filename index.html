Who we are

SavAItale ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application designed to help individuals review and better understand healthcare bills and related information.

What data we store

Data stored locally on your device

All data you enter into SavAItale is stored only on your device. This includes:

  • Information you enter in the app
  • Medical bills and scanned images
  • Notes and tracking information
  • Cost awareness insights

This data does not leave your device unless you choose to share it.

AI-powered features

When you use certain features, selected data may be processed by third-party AI services (Anthropic Claude) to generate insights. Only minimal required data is used.

What we do NOT do

  • No server-side data storage
  • No data selling or sharing
  • No ads or tracking
  • No direct access to your data

Permissions

Camera and storage access are used only to allow document capture and review within the app.

Notifications

All notifications are generated locally on your device.

Important note

SavAItale is a personal health information tool and is not a healthcare provider or insurance service.
