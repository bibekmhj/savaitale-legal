Who we are

SavAItale ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application designed to help individuals understand, track, and dispute medical bills in the United States. SavAItale is operated as an independent application.

What data we collect

Data stored locally on your device

All data you enter into SavAItale is stored exclusively on your device using your phone's local storage. This includes:

  • Your name and insurance information you enter in Settings
  • Medical bills you add manually or via the bill scanner
  • Photos of medical bills you scan
  • Cost estimates you generate
  • Notes and dispute tracking information
  • Your deductible and spending information

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export it.

Data sent to Anthropic (Claude AI)

When you use the Bill Scanner or Cost Estimator features, images and text are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API for analysis. This data is processed according to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We send only the minimum data needed for the feature to work.

We recommend not including sensitive personal identifiers (such as Social Security numbers) in bills you scan, though such information is typically not present on medical bills.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not operate any servers or databases
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not have access to your bills or personal information

Camera and photo library

SavAItale requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to photograph and upload medical bills. Photos are stored locally on your device. We do not upload photos to any server other than sending them to Anthropic's Claude API for bill analysis when you initiate a scan.

Notifications

SavAItale may send you local notifications (reminders for bill due dates and dispute follow-ups). These are scheduled entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to our servers.

HIPAA notice

SavAItale is a consumer-facing personal finance tool, not a covered entity or business associate under HIPAA. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to SavAItale servers. You are responsible for the security of your own device.

Children's privacy

SavAItale is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
